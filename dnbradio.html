Drum & Bass Radio

2021 may have had a turbulent start, but we had something brewing just in time for the long lockdown days.

Taking full advantage of Spotify’s exciting new podcast feature that combines classic playlist navigation with spoken-word commentary, we launched the monthly Drum & Bass Radio via the platform on the 1st January.

During the 1 hour shows that will be going live on the first day of each month, we will be supplying full playlists packed with new talent, hidden gems and unsung heroes – paired with a full-length radio show where a variety of expert hosts and guests from across the industry will be discussing what they’re listening to in depth.

This month, we welcomed Radio 1’s Drum and Bass radio presenter Lauren Charlesworth to kick off proceedings with a deep dive into new and old material from a wealth of names including Netsky, goddard., Shy FX and more.

You can now enjoy conversations from key DnB figureheads alongside those crucial Track IDs every month. Don’t miss out.
